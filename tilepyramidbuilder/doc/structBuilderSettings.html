uint BuilderSettings::output_image_width

Dimensions of the output images. Image not on the bottom level are scaled to fit within these dimensions.

If dividing these dimensions by those of the input image does not yield a powers of 2, the complete image and parts near edges are padded with a single color.

uint BuilderSettings::output_quality

An integer between 0 and 100 indicating the quality of the output images.

Only applicable on lossy compression

rgb_t BuilderSettings::padding_color

The color used for padding.

bool BuilderSettings::use_padding

Whether or not to use padding at all. When true, images that do not fill a whole tile will be padded with padding_byte. When false, images will be cut to size.
